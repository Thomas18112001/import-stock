WEAR MOI, DESIGNER OF DANCE CLOTHES AND BALLET SLIPPERS

Wear Moi is a leading dancewear brand created in 1992 by two professional dancers and now sold in over 60 countries.

Since then, the French ballet brand has provided its customers with the very best in dance fashion with its high-quality products, innovative styles, and unique aesthetic. Ballerinas and male dancers worldwide are fond of Wear Moi leotards, soft ballet shoes, tights, pointe shoes, and uniforms! Passion for Excellence is what gives Wear Moi such distinction in ballet dancewear.

After all, what is better than a brand made by dancers for dancers?
